Introducing "Kaptcha" - the ultimate battle of wit and speed between two aspiring captcha masters!

Kaptcha is a fun game where a user is presented with a basic challenges such as simple mathematical equations, knowledge of geography, meme question, understanding of our professor and much more. While playing this game you will hopefully feel a rush of adrenalin as a the timer slowly decreases singling end of you turn and the WIN/LOSS of your game! For each game you enter you will gamble your trophies as you seek to reach the top! The purpose of a kaptcha is learn new facts and broaden your horizon while also having the time of your life!
